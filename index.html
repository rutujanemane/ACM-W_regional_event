About ACM-W Regional Event

ACM-W Regional Celebration, Pune is going to be a one-day event consisting of several sub-events like keynote speaker program, addresses by esteemed dignitaries, and two competitions: Blog Writing and Poster Making( scroll for the details of these competitions). This year's regional celebration is going to be hosted by PCCoE ACM-W Student Chapter for the first time ever. The event is going to be carried out in collaboration with PCCoE ACM Student Chapter, PCCoE-R, PICT and Cummins CoE ACM-W Student Chapters.


About PCCoE ACM-W

This chapter has been established with an aim to support and motivate the women in computing, and thereby bring about gender equality in this field. Although most of the events are organized for women, but this chapter also arranges events which help in the overall development of all the students. Besides that, ACM-W PCCOE encourages women to participate in the ACM-W Regional Celebrations, Grace Hopper Celebrations and all such similar events which are targeted for girls in the field of computing.
